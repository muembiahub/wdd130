Kinshasa Whitewater Rafting Trips

Kinshasa Whitewater Rafting offers thrilling rafting adventures on the Congo River. Our experienced guides will take you on an unforgettable journey through the rapids, providing an exciting and safe experience for all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect trip for you. Join us for an adventure of a lifetime!

Our Trips

Beginner Rafting Trip

Perfect for first-timers, this trip offers a gentle introduction to whitewater rafting. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the Congo River while navigating mild rapids with our experienced guides.

Intermediate Rafting Trip

For those with some rafting experience, this trip provides a thrilling adventure with moderate rapids. Experience the excitement of the Congo River's dynamic waters.

Advanced Rafting Trip

Designed for experienced rafters, this trip takes you through the most challenging rapids of the Congo River. Get ready for an adrenaline-pumping adventure!
