Kinshasa Whitewater Rafting

Kinshasa Whitewater Rafting offers thrilling rafting adventures on the Congo River. Our experienced guides will take you on an unforgettable journey through the rapids, providing an exciting and safe experience for all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect trip for you. Join us for an adventure of a lifetime!

History

Kinshasa Whitewater Rafting was founded in 2005 by Jonathan M. Muembia, a passionate rafter with a love for adventure. Since then, we have been providing high-quality rafting trips to visitors from around the world. Our team of experienced guides is dedicated to ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for all our guests. We are committed to preserving the natural beauty of the Congo River and promoting sustainable tourism in the region.
